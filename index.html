What is this tool?

This tool helps you create an optimized prompt (question) to ask AI assistants like ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini about Ritsumeikan University's international admissions.

How does it work?

  1. Step 1: Enter your basic information (desired program, country/region, education background)
  2. Step 2: Select what topics you want to know about
  3. Step 3: Review or customize the information sources (optional)
  4. Step 4: Copy the generated prompt and paste it into your preferred AI tool

Why use this tool?

The generated prompt prioritizes official university sources by default. You can also add trusted sources in Step 3 when needed.
